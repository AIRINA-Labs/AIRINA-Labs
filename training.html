What this catalog is not.

This is not a marketplace of every possible AI course. Every program here is tied to AIRINA's own research lineage — topology, interpretability, applied ML for African systems. We do not run programs longer than two weeks. No one graduates without defending. No trainee writes a check. New programs are added only when the underlying research has matured to the point we can teach it honestly.

How a cohort works

From application to certificate, in three movements.

01 · Express interest

Email the program inbox. Short note on your background and what you want to get out of the program. We respond within five working days. For oversubscribed cohorts, we run a one-question selection.

02 · Show up and work

Cohort sizes are small. Mornings are the research-led lecture; afternoons are hands-on lab. Course materials, code, and reading lists are open. Bilingual delivery — switching between EN and FR as the room requires.

03 · Defend

Every program ends with a graded defense — a project, a paper read-out, or a research scoping document, depending on the program. The certificate is earned, not handed out. Sponsoring institutions get a written debrief.
