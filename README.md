## 🌍 Who We Are

**AIRINA Labs** — AI Research and Innovation Nexus for Africa — is a research-driven AI institution headquartered in **Cotonou, Benin Republic**, operating at the intersection of applied research, product development, and human capital.

> We are not a consulting firm. We are not a bootcamp. We are not a technology vendor.
> **We are an institution** — built for the long term, anchored in rigorous research, and committed to African talent and African solutions.

We start in Benin. We do not stop there. Our mission is **continental in scope, universal in ambition, and African at its core.**

---

## ⚡ The Challenge

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 📉 Financial Exclusion at Scale

Over **60% of adults** in West and Central Africa remain outside formal financial systems — no credit, no insurance, no savings infrastructure, no economic mobility.

Financial institutions hold rich data but lack **locally-built AI capacity** to turn it into impact. The models imported from the Global North don't fit African realities.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🎓 The Missing Bridge

Africa produces thousands of STEM graduates every year — mathematicians, statisticians, engineers, computer scientists with real capability.

The vast majority remain **unemployed or severely underemployed** — not for lack of talent, but because **no structured bridge** exists between academic training and the frontier of industry.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

> **AIRINA Labs is that bridge — and the institution on the other side of it.**

## 🔬 Active Research Areas

> Current focus: **AI for Financial Inclusion in West Africa**

| Research Track | Methods & Tools | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 📊 **Credit Scoring for the Unbanked** | Gradient Boosting, Alternative Data, Fairness-Aware ML | 🟢 Active |
| 🔍 **Financial Crisis Detection** | Topological Data Analysis (TDA), Time-Series, Persistence Homology | 🟢 Active |
| 📱 **Mobile Money Fraud Detection** | Graph Neural Networks, Anomaly Detection | 🟡 In Design |
| 🌾 **Agricultural Finance Risk Models** | Geospatial Analysis, Remote Sensing, XGBoost | 🔵 Phase 2 |
