## 🌍 Who We Are

**AIRINA Labs** — AI Research and Innovation Nexus for Africa — is a research-driven AI and Data institution headquartered in **Cotonou, Benin Republic**. We operate at the intersection of applied research, product development, and human capital building.

> We are not a consulting firm. We are not a bootcamp. We are not a technology vendor.
> **We are an institution** — built for the long term, anchored in rigorous research, and committed to the development of African talent and African solutions.

We start in Benin. We do not stop there. Our mission is **continental in scope, universal in ambition, and African at its core.**

---

## ⚡ The Problem We Solve

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 📉 Financial Exclusion
Over **60% of adults** in West and Central Africa remain excluded from formal financial systems — no credit, no insurance, no savings, no economic opportunity.

Financial institutions cannot leverage their data effectively because **local AI talent has no structured pathway** to reach them.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🎓 Talent Without a Bridge
Africa produces thousands of STEM graduates every year — mathematicians, statisticians, engineers, computer scientists.

The vast majority remain **unemployed or underemployed** — not for lack of talent, but because no structured bridge exists between university and industry.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

> **AIRINA Labs exists to close this gap.**
