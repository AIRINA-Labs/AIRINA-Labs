Our values

Four words we have to mean.

Rigor

We hold our research and products to the highest standards we know how to apply. We publish, we validate, and we are honest about what we know and what we do not.

Relevance

Every research question and every product is grounded in the realities of African communities, institutions, and markets. Generic baselines do not count.

Inclusion

We exist for the excluded — financially, economically, and professionally. Work that does not reach those who need it most is not the work we want to do.

Continuity

We are building an institution, not a project. Every decision is made with the next decade in mind.
