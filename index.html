01 — The problem

A double failure with a
single root cause

Africa produces the talent. Africa has the need. What's missing is the institution that bridges both.

01

Financial exclusion at scale

Over 60% of adults in West and Central Africa remain excluded from formal financial systems — no credit, no insurance, no savings. Financial institutions cannot leverage their data effectively because local AI talent has no structured pathway to reach them.

02

Talent without a bridge

Africa produces thousands of STEM graduates every year — mathematicians, statisticians, engineers, computer scientists. The vast majority remain unemployed or underemployed — not for lack of talent, but because no structured bridge exists between university and industry.

AIRINA Labs exists to close this gap — simultaneously advancing African AI knowledge, building local talent, and translating both into practical solutions for African challenges.
02 — What we do

Four integrated pillars

Each pillar is distinct in function — but deeply interconnected. Research informs products. Products generate revenue. Training builds talent. The incubator transforms talent into founders.

01 · Research 🔬

Applied research

Original, applied research on AI, data science, and financial inclusion in Africa. We publish, contribute to global knowledge, and make findings accessible to policymakers and practitioners.

02 · Products 🛠️

AI products

Research translated into practical tools — credit scoring models, fraud detection, customer analytics, and financial access tools — designed specifically for African realities.

03 · Training 🎯

Talent programs

Structured transition programs moving STEM graduates from academic knowledge into industry-ready careers. Participants work on live research and real client problems — learning by doing.

04 · Incubator 🚀

AIRINA Ventures

Supporting STEM graduates who want to build AI-driven companies — with mentorship, technical support, workspace, and institutional network access. Launching Phase 2.

Research informs Products · Products generate credibility & revenue · Training powers both · Incubator transforms talent into founders
