For Individuals

If you want to train, read, or work with us.

AIRINA exists for mathematicians, statisticians, engineers, data scientists, and the kind of trainee who reads the paper before the session. There are three ways in.

Location Cotonou & partner sites Languages EN / FR Cost to trainees Zero
Three ways in

Pick the one that fits where you are.

01 · Take a cohort

Train with us.

Two-day to two-week programs in topological data analysis, interpretable ML, and the mathematical foundations of robust machine learning. Cohorts of twelve. Every program ends with a graded defense. Bilingual delivery. No trainee writes a check.

 02 · Read with us

Join the Paper Club.

A bi-weekly reading group on topological data analysis, interpretable ML, and applied mathematics for African systems. One paper, two discussants, an hour of careful argument. Hybrid: in-person at the Cotonou office or by Zoom. First-timers welcome.

Who this is for

The honest fit.

  • Data scientists and ML engineers at banks, MFIs, telcos — and the people they hire — who need topology and interpretability skills that the standard online catalog does not teach.
  • Graduate students in mathematics, statistics, or computer science, especially from AIMS, partner universities, and the African research diaspora.
  • Self-taught engineers with strong applied chops who want the mathematical foundations they skipped — and who can defend their work in front of researchers.
  • Researchers working at the intersection of topology, geometry, and ML who want a working community on the continent.
  • Not for people looking for a generalist data-science bootcamp or a paid certificate of attendance. The institute does not run either.
