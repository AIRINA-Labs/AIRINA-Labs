Who this is for

The honest fit.

  • Banks and microfinance institutions in the BCEAO zone (and adjacent francophone West Africa) operating real credit, fraud, or risk pipelines on local data.
  • Mobile-money operators and fintechs with transaction-scale data and an interest in interpretable models that pass regulatory review.
  • Foundations and development funders investing in African data-science capacity, applied AI research, or financial-inclusion tooling.
  • Partner universities and research institutes (AIMS, ACAS, university partners) interested in co-authored work or visiting researcher arrangements.
  • Not for institutions looking for off-the-shelf vendor relationships or generic AI consulting. The engagement begins with a real problem, in our office, on the whiteboard.
Charter

What we will not do.

No program runs longer than two weeks. No one graduates without defending. No work stays in a drawer. No trainee writes a check. No quarter is the horizon. These constraints shape how institutional engagements work — the timeline is the research timeline, the deliverable is the research output, and the value to your team is the people they meet in the room.
